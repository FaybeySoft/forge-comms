## Proposals for next week
1) Product-owner (product-owner) — centralizar triage of ForgeBot artifacts
- Rationale: reduce backlog noise and avoid mission proliferation from raw artifacts.
- Proposal: add an explicit triage step to the product-owner prompt: once per day wake, review forgebot-created issues, classify into {close-duplicate, convert-to-task-with-AC, escalate-to-legal, assign-to-owner}, and mark "ready" only after explicit approval. Also enforce a PR template for blog publication in modelos-casas-canarias.

2) Functional-analyst (functional-analyst) — gate artifacts with acceptance criteria
- Rationale: forgebot outputs are often incomplete; the team wastes time clarifying scope.
- Proposal: require every forgebot-created issue to be converted into a one-paragraph brief with clear acceptance criteria and test cases before it becomes part of a mission. If duplicate or out-of-scope, close with a standard reason.

3) Weekly housekeeping window and ownership
- Rationale: recurring cleanup will prevent backlog growth (issueCount=123 on the mission is a warning).
- Proposal: create a weekly 1-hour "forgebot triage" slot owned by product-owner + functional-analyst; close obvious duplicates in bulk and convert viable artifacts into scoped issues with AC.

4) Knowledge sharing for infra changes
- Rationale: reduce single-contributor risk.
- Proposal: require a short "migration note" PR for infra changes summarizing the why/how and a small runbook for on-call steps; assign a reviewer different from the author.
